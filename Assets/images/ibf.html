We help build a better world.

Infobric is a leading provider of digital solutions designed to create a safer, more productive, and sustainable construction industry. We develop smart tools that simplify the complexity of the construction ecosystem, empowering more than 12,000 companies to gain better control, work more efficiently, and ensure stronger compliance.

Trusted by more than 12 000 customers and 450 000 unique users.

Connecting the Construction Ecosystem.

Our technology connects all stakeholders — from owners and developers to general contractor, subcontractors, and individual workers - into a digital ecosystem, fostering better control, increased efficiency and improved compliance. By breaking down silos, we create smarter workflows that increase transparency and accountability across the entire value chain.

General Contractors

Improve safety, streamline workforce management and maximize resource-efficiency through complete control across your construction sites.

Subcontractors

Simplify compliance, enhance safety, and improve your workforce communication and coordination, so you can focus on your delivery.

Developers

Stay on top of safety, contractor qualifications, and site standards while connecting all stakeholders for greater transparency and efficiency.

Workers

Our digital tools empower construction workers with knowledge, structure, and support — all critical in a high-risk, high-demand environment like construction.

Rental companies

Streamline rental operations with real-time control using cloud-based solutions. Centralize rental agreements, fleet availability, and contract management to reduce errors and keep your fleet ready for every job.

Regulators

Simplify compliance with our digital solutions, reducing admin and enhancing transparency and accountability in construction projects.

Financial Institutions

Infobric drives the construction industry towards zero accidents, fraud, and emissions, supporting Sweden’s "Bankinitiativet" against financial crime.

Accelerating Sustainability.

At Infobric, we are dedicated to accelerating the construction industry’s journey to zero accidents, zero fraud, and zero emissions by enabling our customers to radically improve management of their workers, sites and assets with our digital solutions. Through our impact, we aim to elevate industry standards to contribute to a more sustainable construction.
